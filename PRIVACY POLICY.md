Privacy Policy for Hey Camera
Effective date: 26th March, 2026

Hey Camera respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

1. Information and permissions the app uses

Hey Camera may access the following features and data on your device:

Camera to take photos and record videos
Microphone to detect and process voice commands
Photos and videos to save captured media and let you browse your gallery
Local device storage/app storage to store required speech recognition model files and app preferences

The app requests these permissions because they are necessary for its core functionality.

2. How the app uses this data

Hey Camera uses device access only to provide app features, including:

Capturing photos and videos
Recognizing spoken commands
Saving media to your device
Browsing gallery content
Sharing or deleting media when you choose
Downloading and storing speech recognition model files needed for voice control

3. Audio and voice processing

Hey Camera uses microphone access to listen for voice commands.

The app is designed to use on-device speech recognition after the required model files are available on the device. Voice input is used to recognize commands and operate app features. We do not sell or share your voice data.

4. Photos and videos

Photos and videos taken with Hey Camera are stored on your device and in your device gallery, depending on your device settings and permissions.

The app may also display, share, or delete media only when you choose to use those features.

5. Data sharing

Hey Camera does not sell your personal data.

We do not share your photos, videos, or voice input with third parties except when required for functionality you directly request.

6. Internet access

Hey Camera may use network access to download required speech recognition model files or related app resources. After setup, core speech recognition works entirely on device.

7. Data storage

Hey Camera stores certain data locally on your device, such as:

downloaded speech model files
app preferences
captured media saved by you

8. Your choices

You can:

deny permissions, although some app features may not work properly
remove the app at any time
clear app storage in your device settings, which may remove app preferences and downloaded model files

9. Children’s privacy

Hey Camera is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Security

We take reasonable steps to limit data use to what is needed for the app to function. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this policy.

12. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: contact.ertelek@yahoo.com
